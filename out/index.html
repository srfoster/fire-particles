Illusions of Flame

The Illusions of Flame Rune Collection was crafted by Firebeard the Great in the early days of the Dark Invasion. They were instrumental in turning the tide, at least for a while.

The Collection was thought to be lost for many years. Recently, however, I ( Stephen the Scrivener ) stumbled across Firebeard ’s old tomes and have been working diligently to reconstruct the original Runes.
